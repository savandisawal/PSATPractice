Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

PSAT Practice is a personal, family-run study tool that helps students practice for the PSAT. It is not a commercial product and shows no advertising.

Information we collect

When you register, we store the username, email address, and name you provide, along with your practice-test results (answers, scores, and timing). That's all.

How it's used

Your email is used only to sign you in and to notify you when your account is approved. Test results are stored so you can review your progress on your dashboard. We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties, and we do not use analytics or advertising services.

Third-party content

Pages load fonts and libraries from public CDNs (Google Fonts, jsDelivr, unpkg) and the in-test calculator is provided by Desmos. These services may see standard web request data (such as your IP address) when the content loads.

Account deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, email disawalsavan@gmail.com and it will be removed.

Contact

Questions about this policy: disawalsavan@gmail.com
